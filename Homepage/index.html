About Me

“There is no magic to achievement. It is really about hard work, choices and persistence.” – Michelle Obama

My name is Pragati and I am an aspiring technical writer. 

I was introduced to technical writing in my last job at MaaS360 (now acquired by IBM). While I explored it by chance, I enjoyed developing different types of technical and business documentation. After moving to the Bay area in 2015, I joined an education non-profit Code for fun where I managed curriculum development efforts. Working with children, I learnt:

  1. Journey of learning is infinite. 
  2. Resilience is important and makes us multi-tenant 
  3. Teaching is my new hobby. 
  4. Hope. Most powerful word in my dictionary.
  5. Technical writing - I miss you!

In January 2019, I joined the technical writing certification program at San Jose State University (SJSU), San Jose, Califoria, USA to broaden my writing skills. While I await for my next technical writing gig, I work with different non-profits to practice my technical and writing skills.
